Before Class:

  • Bring the following pattern pieces to class:
    • Hand Sewing Stitches Pattern
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Hand Sewing Needles
    • All-purpose thread in a DARK color
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Clear ruler with 1/8 inch markings
    • Frixion brand pen(s)
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Transfer Paper
    • Serrated Transfer Wheel
    • Tape Measure
    • Thread snips/embroidery scissors (optional)

During Class:

  • Learn and practice basic hand-sewn stitches necessary for garment construction, alteration and repair.

After Class:

  • Complete the following: Running stitches, backstitches, whip stitches (seam repair), ladder stitches (seam repair).
