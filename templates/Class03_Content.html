Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Traditional drawing supplies of choice
    • Consider bringing tracing paper or contact paper to duplicate sketches

During Class:

  • Choose one character from our script and develop a costume design for this character.
  • Using traditional drawing tools, begin to sketch this character.
  • Practice drawing different poses for your chosen character.
  • Begin developing costume ideas for your character.
  • Practice using different traditional media.

After Class:

  • Finalize your character's costume design using traditional media.
